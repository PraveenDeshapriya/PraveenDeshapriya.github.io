DESSERTS-WATTALAPAN

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz. palm sugar , grated
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • Â½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 oz. cashew nuts , roasted unsalted,

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 300F/150C.
  2. Step 2: In a saucepan, add the palm sugar, coconut milk, spices and vanilla.
  3. Step 3: Heat the pan on low heat, and stir for 5 minutes, until the sugar dissolves.Reserve and let cool, stirring occasionally.
  4. Step 4: When the mixture is lukewarm, break the eggs in a large salad bowl and whisk until they are pale and creamy.
  5. Step 5: Gradually add the coconut milk mixture and whisk for 2 minutes.Filter the mixture through a fine sieve.
  6. Step 6: Divide the dish into 4 ramekins and place in a large, shallow oven dish.
  7. Step 7: Fill this dish with boiling water to get to the middle of the ramekins.
  8. Step 8: Bake in a bain-marie (water bath) for 30 minutes.Sprinkle half of the cashews and cook for another 10 minutes.
  9. Step 9: Refrigerate for 3 hours before tasting.Sprinkle each ramekin with the remaining cashews just before serving.
