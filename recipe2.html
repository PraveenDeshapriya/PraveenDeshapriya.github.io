MAIN DISHES-PARIPPU CURRY

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 2 cloves garlic , chopped
  • 2 onions , chopped
  • 4 curry leaves , thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1¼ cup water
  • ½ lime
  • 1 green hot pepper
  • Salt

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Wash the lentils well by changing the water at least three times.
  2. Step 2: Boil them in the water with the saffron, chili powder, turmeric, curry leaves and cloves.
  3. Step 3: After about 15 minutes, when the color of the lentils changes from red to yellow, remove from the heat and pour into a large bowl.
  4. Step 4: Heat the oil in a very large skillet over medium heat. Add the mustard, fenugreek, and cumin seeds and fry for 1 minute.
  5. Step 5: Add the onion and garlic and mix well for 20 seconds.
  6. Step 6: Add the boiled lentils and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Add the coconut milk, curry powder and salt to taste. Cook until the curry begins to boil.
  8. Step 8: Remove from heat and add the lime juice.
  9. Step 9: Add the raw or fried green hot pepper on top (optional).
  10. Step 10: Serve hot with rice or bread.
